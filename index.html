✦ Features

Everything an explorer needs

🎯

Physics Bounce

Realistic ludo-style token drops from the top of the screen with multi-bounce physics and rotation.

🗺

Real Geocoding

Every landed point is reverse-geocoded via OpenStreetMap — real place names, districts, and coordinates.

🛰

3 Map Layers

Switch between Street, Satellite, and Terrain views. Each layer tells a different story about the land.

👥

Multiplayer Mode

Launch 2–8 colored tokens simultaneously for a race across the map. Perfect for group play.

🎡

Spin Wheel

Spin a wheel to pick a terrain category before your bounce — add a layer of luck to exploration.

⭐

Save Favorites

Loved a spot? Save it. Build a personal atlas of discovered places from all your sessions.

🔊

Sound Effects

Satisfying bounce and landing sounds using the Web Audio API. Every landing feels like a win.

📱

Mobile Friendly

Fully responsive design. Tap the map on your phone to place tokens anywhere in the selected country.
